A different planet is my first game to be uploaded to the internet. It was created in my spare time during two weeks for the Beginners Circle Jam #3.

All assets have been made by me from the ground up except for the music and some of the sound effects

The goal of the game is to find a different planet in a galaxy filled with boredom and contempt.

It plays as a plataformer in wich you must visit all the planets to open the gate to the next sector, while accounting for the gravity of the different celestial bodies.
